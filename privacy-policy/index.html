Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

1. Introduction

Fortnite Checker ("the App") is a local application that allows you to view your Fortnite account information securely. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

This is a local, client-side application. Your data is not sent to external servers (except through Epic Games' OAuth services).

2. What Information We Collect

Authentication Information

When you log in with Epic Games, we receive:

  • Your Epic Games account ID
  • Your display name
  • Your email address (from Epic Games)
  • OAuth access token (used to fetch your data)

Fortnite Account Data

When you authorize access, we retrieve:

  • Battle royale statistics (wins, kills, matches played)
  • Your cosmetics/locker items
  • V-Bucks balance
  • Battle pass progress
  • Account level and performance metrics
Note: We only access data that you explicitly authorize through Epic Games OAuth. Epic Games controls what information they share with us.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively to:

  • Display your Fortnite account statistics
  • Show your cosmetics and locker items
  • Verify your identity via Epic Games
  • Maintain your authenticated session

We do not: Sell, trade, share, or distribute your personal information to third parties.

4. Data Storage & Security

Local Storage

This application is self-hosted and runs locally on your device. Your data is stored in-memory within your session. When you close the application or clear your session, your data is deleted.

Key Points:
  • No data is permanently stored on external servers
  • Your OAuth tokens are kept in server memory only
  • Closing the application clears all session data
  • Each session is independent and separate

Security Measures

  • HTTPS/SSL for all communications (when deployed)
  • OAuth 2.0 authentication protocol
  • Client secret is never exposed to the browser
  • Access tokens are server-side only

5. Third-Party Services

Epic Games

This application uses Epic Games OAuth for authentication. When you log in:

  • You are redirected to Epic Games' secure login page
  • Epic Games controls your authentication credentials
  • We receive an access token to fetch your data
  • Your password is never shared with us

Epic Games Privacy Policy: https://www.epicgames.com/site/en-US/privacypolicy

Fortnite API

We use Epic Games' public Fortnite APIs to retrieve your account statistics. This data is accessed only when you request it and only stored temporarily during your session.

6. Cookies & Sessions

The App uses session cookies for authentication purposes only:

  • Session ID (to maintain your login during active use)
  • CSRF token (to prevent unauthorized requests)

These cookies are essential for the App to function. You can clear them by logging out or closing the application.

7. Data Deletion & User Rights

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View what data we have about you
  • Delete: Log out to immediately clear all session data
  • Revoke Access: Disconnect the app from Epic Games at any time
  • Opt-Out: Stop using the application

How to Delete Data

  1. Click "Logout" in the application
  2. All session data is immediately deleted
  3. Optional: Revoke app access in your Epic Games account settings

8. Data Retention

Session Data: Deleted when you log out or close the application.

No Permanent Records: We do not maintain backups or permanent records of your Fortnite data.

Logs: Server logs may retain technical information (IP, requests) for debugging, but no personal data is stored.

9. Children's Privacy

This application is intended for users 13 years and older. If you are under 13, please do not use this application without parental consent. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.
